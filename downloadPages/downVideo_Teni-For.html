TENI - FOR YOU (Official Video) ft. Davido

Dr Dolor Entertainment act, Teni has unleashed a new jam on which she christened this song ''For You'' enlisting Nigerian Global musician, Davido, DMW (Davido Music Worldwide) Records CEO on this tune. Which is a potential hit, good lyrics, nice vocals from these two artistes, as Davido murdered this song with his unique style of music. Produced by Pheelz. Teni started gained prominence in the year 2017, after releasing her single hit ''Fargin'', She made her breakthrough after dropping this hits which were a very huge airplay in Nigerian and many other countries namely Case , Uyo Meyo and Askamaya as ''Uyo Meyo'' won Best Recording of the year at The Headies Awards (2019) ''Case'' won Best Pop single at same Headies Awards (2019). She also dropped an Extended Play tagged ''The Quarantine Playlist'' featuring DJ Neptune consisting of 4 soundtracks in the year 2020. Davido real name David Adeleke is a Nigerian-American singer, songwriter and record producer, who is the CEO of Top and Trending record label in Nigerian known as, DMW to which acts, Mayor of Lagos (Mayorkun), The Vibesman (Peruzzi), Yonda and his new signee (Liya) a female act. OBO, dropped an album at the early ending of last year 2020 titled A Better Time consisting of 17 tracks, as he involves both Local and international artiste namely Nick Minaj, Chris Brown, Mayorkun, Bella Shmurda and many more. However, This song comes after Teni announced a few weeks ago via her Instagram page that she is dropping a song with Davido, which follows up with a video directed by Nigerian videographer, Dammy Twitch Just recently, after the whole deed, Teni Makanaki decides to open this song ''For You'' featuring Davido produced by Pheelz, which is nowhere for you to enjoy.
